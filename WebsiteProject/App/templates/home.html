About

Discover the world of herbs with our herbal classification website. Explore, learn, and understand the unique properties and benefits of various herbs, all categorized for easy access and deeper insights into natural remedies.

How

To get started, click the 'Get Started' button on the homepage, which takes you to the upload page. There, you can upload images of herbs either through file upload or directly using your camera. Once the upload is complete, a results container will pop up, displaying the classification details of the herb.
